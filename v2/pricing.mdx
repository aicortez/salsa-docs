## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Pricing Questions

<Accordion title="Are there any hidden fees?">
No hidden fees. All costs are transparent and outlined in our pricing plans.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can I change my plan anytime?">
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Do you offer discounts for non-profits?">
Yes, we offer 25% discounts for qualified non-profit organizations. Contact our sales team for more information.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my plan limits?">
We'll notify you when you're approaching limits and offer options to upgrade or purchase additional capacity.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Is there a setup fee?">
No setup fees for any plan. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden costs.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How is Enterprise pricing determined?">
Enterprise pricing is based on the number of locations, users, integrations, and specific requirements. Contact our sales team for a custom quote.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Do you offer volume discounts?">
Yes, we offer volume discounts for large restaurant chains. Discounts start at 10+ locations.

Yes, Enterprise customers can have custom contracts with specific terms and conditions.
